Jianshan Notes (jianshan.co, hereinafter "this site") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this site.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this site, certain information may be collected automatically through the following means:

- **Google Analytics**: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data, including page views, visit duration, geographic location (country/region level), and device type. For details, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google AdSense**: This site may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences through [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).
- **Server Logs**: Our hosting provider may collect standard server log information, including IP addresses, browser type, and access times.

### Information We Do Not Actively Collect

This site does not require user registration and does not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information (unless you voluntarily contact us via email).

## Use of Cookies

This site uses cookies to provide the following functions:

- **Analytics Cookies**: Collect anonymous visit data through Google Analytics
- **Advertising Cookies**: Display personalized ads through Google AdSense
- **Functional Cookies**: Remember your preference settings (such as dark/light mode)

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

## Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review their terms.

This site transmits data via HTTPS encryption to protect your browsing security. We do not store any personal sensitive information from users.
