### About ValueScope

ValueScope is an open-source AI-powered DCF valuation tool. The foundation is a standardized 10-year free cash flow model (FCFF + WACC + terminal value + sensitivity analysis) — fixed framework, reproducible results. The AI layer helps analyze company fundamentals and suggest valuation parameters, but the final decision is always yours.

Fully open source: [GitHub](https://github.com/alanhewenyu/ValueScope)
